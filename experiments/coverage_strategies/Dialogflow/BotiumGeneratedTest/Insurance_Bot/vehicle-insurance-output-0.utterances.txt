Motor insurance policy covers the policyholder in case of financial losses, resulting from an accident or other damages sustained by the insured vehicle. 

What's included?
✔️ Accidents, Personal Accident Cover
✔️ Fire & Explosion
✔️ Theft
✔️ Calamities

What’s not included?
✔️ Depreciation
✔️ Electrical & Mechanical Breakdown
✔️ Illegal Driving
